Frogbound: The Legend of Sir Hopper

Leap into a lush, hand-crafted kingdom where a brave frog knight sets out to break a mysterious curse. Frogbound blends whimsical storytelling, tight platforming, and strategic combat in a vibrant action-adventure coming soon from Stomaco Studio.

Gameplay Highlights

Amphibian Knight Combat

Swap between swordplay, tongue lash, and shield bash combos to counter quirky foes ranging from spectral mosquitos to corrupted swamp royals.

Metroidvania Exploration

Unlock new traversal abilities like lily-pad gliding and bubble diving to revisit areas, uncover hidden shrines, and forge your own path.

Charming Storytelling

Meet a cast of eccentric bogfolk allies, unravel the legend of Sir Hopper, and decide the fate of a kingdom trapped between myth and muck.

Moody Swamp Atmosphere

Original soundtrack, painterly pixel art, and dynamic weather breathe life into every marsh, grotto, and moonlit lily field you cross.

Step Into the Whispering Mire

Sir Hopper’s Journey

Awaken as the kingdom’s reluctant hero and embark across forgotten ruins, fungal caverns, and skybound pagodas. Each biome teems with secrets, optional bosses, and lore-rich murals waiting to be deciphered.

Collect ancient sigils to upgrade your knightly techniques, brew alchemical tonics with swamp sages, and rescue lost villagers to rebuild a bustling refuge.

  • Dynamic day/night cycles influence enemy behavior.
  • Branching quests reward curiosity and experimentation.
  • Dialogue choices shape Sir Hopper’s legend.
